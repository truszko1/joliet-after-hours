Privacy Policy

Who we are. “Joliet After-Hours Intake” is a small service operated by an individual for the purpose of testing a 7-night after-hours answering pilot for contractors in Will County, Illinois.

What we collect

  • Contact details: name, company, email, phone.
  • Optional notes you share in forms or emails.

How we use your info

  • To respond to your inquiry and set up a pilot.
  • To send daily call summaries during an active pilot.

Legal basis

Our basis is your consent and our legitimate interest in providing the service you requested.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. If you start a pilot, we may share caller details with the contractor you designate solely to deliver the service.

Retention

Lead inquiries are kept up to 90 days. Pilot records may be retained up to 12 months for accounting and dispute resolution.

Your choices

Email us to access, correct, or delete your info. You can opt out of emails at any time.

Security

We use reputable processors (e.g., Google, Stripe, Twilio) with TLS encryption. No method is 100% secure.
